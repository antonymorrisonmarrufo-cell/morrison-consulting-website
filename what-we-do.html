Nine service areas

We work across every area of local government — from transformation and digital strategy to housing, adult social care, commissioning, leadership, and customer experience. We also have a unique in-house design team that creates rich pictures and infographics used by councils and government bodies across the UK and internationally.

Our consultants are senior practitioners, not generalists. Every person we put on an engagement has direct experience in that service area, often as a former director or head of service.

How we work with you

We offer four ways to engage, depending on what you need:

01

Consulting

We scope, design, and deliver a defined piece of work. You get a named senior consultant, a clear brief, and a fixed timeline.

02

Critical friend

We sit alongside your leadership team to challenge, support, and unblock. Not an audit — a partnership.

03

Resourcing

We place a senior associate into your team for a defined period. They bring sector expertise and hit the ground running.

04

Capability building

Through the PSTA, we train your people to do the work themselves. Masterclasses, development programmes, and action learning.

Procurement frameworks

We are on 10+ national procurement frameworks including G-Cloud, ESPO 664, Digital Outcomes and Specialists, Bloom, Constellia, NHS SBS, Healthtrust Europe, and the Office of Government Procurement (Ireland). This means you can engage us quickly, compliantly, and without a full tender process.

For framework enquiries and lot details, email hello@redquadrant.com.
