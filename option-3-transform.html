The RedQuadrant Way

Every consultancy says they
have a methodology.

Most of them mean “we have a slide deck.” Ours is different because it has been tested across 100+ council engagements since 2009, refined with every one, and taught to ~2,000 practitioners through the PSTA.

01

Discover

Understand the system as it actually works — not as the org chart says. Surfaces the real problems, not symptoms.

02

Design

Solutions co-designed with the people who deliver the service, not for them. Tested against real constraints.

03

Deliver

We stay in the room for delivery. Not handing over a report and leaving. Making the design work when it meets reality.

04

Embed

Change that depends on the consultant is not change. We embed new ways of working that survive after we leave.

05

Transfer

Knowledge transfer through PSTA and structured handover. Our goal is to make ourselves unnecessary.
